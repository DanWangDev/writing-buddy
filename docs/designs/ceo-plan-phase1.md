## Vision

### 10x Check
The 10x version isn't just a writing coach — it's a writing *companion* that knows each student deeply. It remembers what techniques they've mastered, adjusts difficulty dynamically, connects vocabulary learning to creative expression, and produces a portfolio that tells the story of a child becoming a writer. Parents don't just see scores — they see their child's voice emerging. The platform supports 3-4 apps with unified subscription, and the AI coaching approach becomes a reusable pattern for any subject that benefits from iterative feedback (maths problem-solving, comprehension analysis, creative projects).

### Key Strategic Decision: Coach-First Sequencing
The original design doc combined coaching engine + platform infrastructure in Phase 1. This review resequenced into:
- **Phase 1a:** Standalone coaching app (own auth) — validate the product differentiator
- **Phase 1b:** Extract shared-auth, wire SSO, add subscription infrastructure
Rationale: The coaching engine is the riskiest unknown. If it doesn't feel human, the platform plumbing is premature.

## Accepted Scope (added to Phase 1a)
- Rubric scoring: structured JSON scores (content, organization, vocabulary, grammar, spelling) stored per coaching pass
- Before/after diff view: word-level diff component shown at submission completion
- Writing streaks: consecutive-day tracking in writing_progress table + flame icon on dashboard
